🛠️
The Action

App Functions

The bridge between the LLM and your app's logic. It allows the AI to execute real-world tasks—like booking a flight or updating a database—rather than just describing how to do it.

🎓
The Knowledge

Skills

Specialized capabilities that give the AI domain-specific expertise. Skills wrap functions into a logical "persona," ensuring higher accuracy for complex, niche industry tasks.

🗺️
The Logic

Journeys

The orchestration layer. Journeys turn a single prompt into a multi-step goal, guiding the user through a sequence of actions from start to finish without friction.

📱
The Engine

Gemma Local

Bringing open-weight models directly to the device. This ensures instant latency, total data privacy, and the ability to operate offline for core AI tasks.
